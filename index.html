Message from the Director

Welcome to TWIBC

"This is the Lord's doing, and it is marvelous in our eyes." — Psalm 118:23

Greetings in the precious name of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ! With a grateful heart, I welcome you to Thy Word International Bible College Bataan. By God's grace and faithfulness, what was once a vision has now become a growing institution committed to training men and women for the work of the ministry.

As an institution aligned with the Assemblies of God, we hold firmly to the authority of the Scriptures and the empowerment of the Holy Spirit. Our passion is to prepare Spirit-filled leaders who will proclaim Christ, serve the church, and impact communities both locally and globally.

TWIBC offers programs such as Certificate in Ministry, and Bachelor in Christian Ministry, designed to equip students with biblical knowledge, spiritual maturity, and practical skills for effective service.
