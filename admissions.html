Admissions & Enrollment

Requirements · Process · Fees · Payment

Before You Begin

Enrollment Requirements

To complete your enrollment at TWIBC, please prepare the following documents and requirements before proceeding to registration.

🪪
Valid ID

Any government-issued or school-issued ID. This will be used to verify your identity during the registration process.

💳
Tuition Fee

A down payment of 50% of the total tuition fee is required upon enrollment. The remaining balance may be settled in installments.

⛪
Pastor's Recommendation

Students are encouraged to enroll with the recommendation of their Senior Pastor. Provide your pastor's name during registration.

How to Enroll

Step-by-Step Admission Process

1
Register & Create Your Account

Fill out the Student Registration Form to receive your Student ID. Your Student ID will serve as your username for the Student Portal. Click the "Register Now" button at the bottom of this page to get started.

→
2
Log In to the Student Portal

Once registered, use your Student ID and the password you created to log in to the Student Portal. Navigate to the Student Log-In page from the main navigation.

→
3
Check Available Subjects & Schedules

Browse through the available subjects and class schedules within the portal. Choose the subjects that align with your program (CCM, CCM Evening, or BCM) and your preferred schedule.

→
4
Enroll in Your Chosen Subjects

Click the "Enroll" button for each subject you wish to take this semester. Review your selections before confirming your enrollment.

→
5
Proceed to Payment

After confirming your subjects, proceed to payment. A down payment of 50% is required. See the payment methods below and send proof of payment to our Facebook page.
