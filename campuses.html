Our Global Campuses

Bataan · Qatar · Europe · Canada

Reaching the Nations

Spirit-Empowered Training Worldwide

Thy Word International Bible College extends beyond the shores of Bataan, Philippines — equipping servants of God wherever they are called. Our campuses span four locations across Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and North America, united by one mission: to train Spirit-empowered ministers of the Gospel.

Whether on-site or online, each campus delivers the same rigorous biblical education under the oversight of Rev. Orlando N. Acda and the TWI leadership team.
