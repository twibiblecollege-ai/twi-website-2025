25 Years of God's Faithfulness

The 25th Anniversary Celebration and Grand Alumni Homecoming of Thy Word International Bible College – Bataan, held on December 10, 2025, was a joyful testament to God's faithfulness through the years.

Alumni who were once students in Bataan's classrooms — and those who studied online from across the world — gathered together as one family in Christ. It was a reunion not merely of former students, but of brothers and sisters in ministry, united by the same calling and the same Lord.

The event was marked by heartfelt worship, testimonies of transformation, and a shared vision for the next chapter of TWIBC's mission to raise Spirit-empowered ministers for the harvest.
